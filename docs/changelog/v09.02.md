# v9.2 — Crowding test override

## Usage

Append `?crowdtest=h` to the URL to force all station crowding dots red:

  http://127.0.0.1:8080/index.html?crowdtest=h

Valid values: `l` (green), `m` (amber), `h` (red), `na` (no dot).

A pink "CROWD TEST: h" badge appears at the bottom-middle of the screen
while the override is active. Tap the badge to clear the override and
reload the page to normal operation.

## What it's for

Lets you visually confirm the crowding rendering works for all three
levels without waiting for real peak-hour data.

Example verification sequence (~60 seconds):

  1. Open normally:  127.0.0.1:8080/index.html
     Pick a route through Dhoby Ghaut → dots may be green (low) or absent
  2. Force red:      127.0.0.1:8080/index.html?crowdtest=h
     Same route → all dots red with glow
  3. Force amber:    127.0.0.1:8080/index.html?crowdtest=m
     Same route → all dots amber
  4. Force green:    127.0.0.1:8080/index.html?crowdtest=l
     Same route → all dots green
  5. Tap the pink badge → back to normal operation
